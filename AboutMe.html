About me

I am currently pursuing my masters in Computer Science specializing in Computing Systems at Georgia Institute of Technology. And prior to starting my graduate studies I was working for four years in the Software industry. Although my work experience was related to Computing Systems, I had done few web development projects as well.

Why Front End Development ?

During my undergraduate studies I had taken a course on web development and was very much fascinated with the simplicity involved in providing a rich user experience for the billions of web users. Naturally I chose to do my UG project related to Web development and developed a mobile web application for making online purchases. Front end is the interface through which billions of users interact with the complex inter connected network, the web. The more simpler and feature rich the front end, the more people would like to visit the site repeatedly. Thus front end developers woud get more opportunities to use their creatvity and innovate with every page they develop in order to attract more people to visit their sites.

Why I built the site this way ?

Front end is all about being creative and using the latest web technologies to create beautiful web pages. Llamas are furry and cute. The current CEO of Zappos is fond of llamas and has two of them as his pets. As this site is developed for getting an internship at Zappos, the site should be reflective of the interests of Zappos's senior most executive.
